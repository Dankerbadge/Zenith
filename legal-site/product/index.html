Move. Track. Grow.

Zenith helps you train without guesswork. You can see what counted today, what changed this week, and what to do next if momentum drops.

What Zenith does today

  • Logs runs, workouts, nutrition, hydration, weight, and active rest
  • Settles each day into clear Winning Day criteria
  • Maintains streak rules with transparent behavior
  • Shows trend context without misleading averages

Who it’s for

Welcome users

  • Casual users building better daily consistency
  • Professionals with structured training blocks
  • Anyone who wants clear rules and low-friction logging

What it’s not

  • Not a public leaderboard
  • Not a generic motivation machine
  • Not a social feed

Together

  • Build accountability by yourself or with friends
  • Understand your body through stable trend context
  • Improve your health with repeatable daily actions

What you can expect

  • Fast logging flows that stay usable on busy days
  • Clear daily outcomes without hidden scoring behavior
  • Support paths that tell you exactly what to try next
