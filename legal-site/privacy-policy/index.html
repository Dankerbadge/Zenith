Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

1. What this policy covers

This policy explains what data Zenith collects, why we collect it, and what controls you have.

2. Data we collect

  • Account data: email and profile fields you provide.
  • Product data: workouts, nutrition logs, hydration, weight, and streak-related records.
  • Community data: username/handle, friend relationships, teams/groups, and content you choose to post.
  • Optional integration data: health/workout context when you enable device and wearable permissions (e.g., Apple Health/HealthKit, Apple Watch, Garmin watch/Connect IQ).
  • Support data: issue details you choose to send to support.

3. How we use data

  • Operate core app functionality.
  • Provide trend views and consistency logic.
  • Resolve support issues and product bugs.
  • Protect system integrity and prevent abuse.

4. What we do not do

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not run ad-targeting behavior based on personal health logs.
  • We do not claim medical diagnosis capabilities.

5. Permissions and optional data

Permissions such as Location, Health, and Notifications are explained in detail on our Data & Permissions page.

Where a permission is optional, core app behavior remains available without it.

6. Data retention and deletion

We retain data only as needed for app operation, legal compliance, and support handling.

You can request export or deletion through app settings or by contacting support@zenithfit.app.

7. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. No system can be guaranteed perfectly secure.

8. Third-party services

Zenith relies on selected third-party services for infrastructure, payments, and diagnostics. Those providers process data under their own terms and security controls.

  • Supabase (authentication and cloud data for community features).
  • Apple HealthKit / Apple Watch frameworks (when you enable Apple Health or Apple Watch features).
  • Garmin Connect IQ (when you enable Garmin watch companion features).
  • RevenueCat (subscriptions / purchases, if monetization features are enabled in your build).

9. Policy updates

If this policy changes materially, we will update this page and revise the date above.

10. Contact

Privacy questions: privacy@zenithfit.app

Support: support@zenithfit.app
