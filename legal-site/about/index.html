About Zenith

Built as a response to noisy fitness apps.

Zenith was built for people who want structure without performance theater. We care about consistency, clear rules, and repeatable behavior.

What we value

  • Truth over reward inflation
  • Clarity over motivational pressure
  • Continuity over intensity spikes
  • Privacy by default

What we won’t do

No attention economy loops

No likes. No vanity rankings. No engagement traps dressed as accountability.

No hidden rule changes

If a rule affects streaks or outcomes, it is visible and explained.

No fake confidence

When data is sparse, we say so. We do not invent precision.

No subscription coercion

Core consistency workflows stay usable in the free tier.

What this means in practice

You can miss a day, return the next day, and continue with intact trust in the system. That is the product.
