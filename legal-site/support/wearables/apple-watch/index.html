Apple Watch support

We are building watch support carefully. Workout control has to be reliable before we mark this live.

Current status

In development

Apple Watch recording and remote control flows are in active development and not yet released to users.

What works today

  • iOS workout logging and run summaries in the Zenith app
  • Daily Winning Day settlement and streak behavior
  • Post-run review and stats trend updates on phone

What is being built

  • Watch-first start, pause, resume, and end control
  • Safe end confirmation to prevent accidental run stop
  • Companion sync reliability when phone is locked or backgrounded
No pricing is shown on wearable pages. Subscription management remains inside the Zenith mobile app.
