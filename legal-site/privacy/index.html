Privacy overview

Short version first. Formal policy is linked below.

What we collect

  • Account basics (email and profile fields)
  • Logs you create (workouts, meals, hydration, weight)
  • Community data you choose to create (username, friends, teams, posts)
  • Optional device and wearable data when you turn integrations on (e.g. Apple Health, Apple Watch, Garmin watch/Connect IQ)

What we don’t do

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not run attention-feed ad targeting.
  • We do not pretend to collect less than we actually use.

Why we collect it

To run core product functions: logging, streak logic, trend views, and support diagnostics when requested.

Your controls

Use in-app settings or support to request export or deletion.
